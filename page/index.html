The Insight Program

Insight is a fellowship program, designed to help transition people out of academia and into industry. The program runs across 12 weeks, during which fellows design a project, and create a minimum viable product. This isn't designed to be a final polished product ready to present to a customer, but something that demonstrates a proof of concept and the skills of the fellows.

My project was What2Lego, a Lego project recommender. The concept was simple - upload a photo of some Legos to the web app, and it will locate the legos in the images, identify them, and suggest a set out of a catalog to build with them. While easily described, each step presented it's own challenge.

For an overview, including all the results, see my video description of the project on youtube.
